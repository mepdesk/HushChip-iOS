Mockup 02
Expandable Feature Grid
Same six cells. Click any cell to reveal one extra sentence of detail. Adds trust depth without adding scroll length. Click the cells below to try.
Replaces the existing "Why HushChip" grid in place
+
Discreet
No branding. No logos. No crypto references. The card reveals nothing.
It looks like a hotel key card. Sits in your wallet next to bank cards, invisible. Nobody knows what it holds unless you tell them.
+
Affordable
Under £10 including UK shipping. A fraction of the alternatives.
SeedKeeper cards from Satochip cost £21+. Metal seed plates start at £15. HushChip gives you EAL6+ encryption for less than a takeaway.
+
Secure
EAL6+ certified secure element. PIN protected. Locks permanently after failed attempts.
The same chip class used in passports and banking cards. Your PIN is verified on the chip itself. No software, no server, no backdoor.
+
Open Source
Every line of code is on GitHub. AGPLv3 licensed. Verify it yourself.
The applet is an unmodified fork of a public repository. No hidden code. No telemetry. You can compile it from source and flash it yourself.
+
UK Based
Ships from the UK. Fast delivery. No customs. No hassle.
Alternatives ship from Belgium or China with customs fees and delays. HushChip posts next day via Royal Mail in a plain envelope.
+
Offline
No accounts. No cloud. No data collection. Your secrets never touch the internet.
The app communicates with your card via NFC only. No WiFi. No Bluetooth. No server calls. Even the app works without an internet connection.
